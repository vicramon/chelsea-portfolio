About Me
I’m a Product Designer in Los Angeles with 0-1 startup experience across AI and data-heavy products. I help teams turn ambiguity into clarity, align on the right problems, and design solutions that scale.
My Approach
Identify
I turn vague business goals into crisp problem statements and actionable design directions.
Facilitate
I facilitate discovery by asking the right questions to uncover real problems before jumping to solutions.
Align
I align cross-functional stakeholders around shared goals, success metrics, and decision criteria.
Execute
I translate complex ideas into intuitive, scalable products that early users could adopt.
Collaboration Fuels It All
What I love most is the collaboration – navigating different perspectives, clarifying the problem space, and bringing entire teams into alignment before opening Figma.
My Life Outside of Design
I’m more than a designer, I’m also an ESFP personality type, an adventure seeker, a proud cat mom of 2 (Millie and Nemo), a lover of crafts and making art, an amateur cake baker, and a HUGE fan of Halloween and spooky things!
